Fried Rice

Description

A combination of eggs rice and vegetables to make a nice side dish or a balanced meal

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 270 Grams(dry) of any rice (leftover white rice works best)
  • 1 Large carrot diced
  • 1 Bunch of green onions, diced
  • 1 Cup of frozen peas, or fresh
  • 3 TBSP butter/margarine
  • 3 TBSP high heat cooking oil (canola, vegetable, peanut)
  • 1 TBSP (14g) white sugar
  • 3 TBSP (42g) soy sauce
  • 1 TBSP (12g) white vinegar
  • 3 Cloves minced garlic (powdered can work too)
  • 1 TBSP grated ginger (powdered can work too)

Steps

  1. Scramble 3 eggs over medium heat, using 3 TBSP of butter to grease the pan, finely chop into small pieces
  2. Cook the 270g of rice according to the directions on the bag, or grab the equivalent leftover rice you have
  3. Allow cooked rice to cool adequately, using it hot works but yields better results cold
  4. set cooked eggs and rice to the side for the moment
  5. Using 3 TBSP of high heat oil, saute the diced carrots over medium-high heat until cooked thoroughly
  6. Add the green onions at the tail end of the process
  7. Add 1 cup of peas to the mix, cook to desired pea tenderness
  8. Combine sugar, vinegar, soy sauce, ginger and garlic together and whisk thoroughly
  9. Add white rice and fry until oil is completely incorporated into rice
  10. Switch off heat, then add your sauce until mixed
  11. Last to go in is the eggs, so you don't overcook them
  12. Add sesame oil or salt if you so desire
