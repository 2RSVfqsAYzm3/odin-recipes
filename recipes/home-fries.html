Home Fries

Description

Russet potato julienned, parboiled and fried to give a restaraunt style fry in the comfort of your home

Ingredients

  • 400-600 Grams of Russet potatoes (can use red or yellow too)
  • 1 TBPS Oil (olive, canola, vegetable)
  • Salt or seasonings of your choice

Steps

  1. Wash potatoes under cold water to remove any dirt left over
  2. Cut in half vertically, then cut those sections in half again, cut thinner if desired
  3. Once potatoes are the desired thickness, boil on low-medium heat until you can sink a fork/knife into a piece easily
  4. carefully strain the potatoes, and toss in a oil of your choice
  5. Add salt and any other seasonings, cook at 400C for 25-30 min, flipping halfway
  6. Let cool for 5 min before serving
  7. These can be cooked on a tray in the oven or in a air-fryer
