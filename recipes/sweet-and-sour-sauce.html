Sweet and sour sauce

Description

A very simple sauce used for a variety of foods, asian dishes, meatballs, any breaded meat etc.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 Cup White Sugar
  • 2/3 Cup Water
  • 1/3 Cup Vinegar
  • 1/4 Cup Soy Sauce
  • 2 TBSP Cornstarch
  • 1 TBSP Ketchup

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients besides cornstarch into a pot and let it heat to a boil
  2. Reduce heat and simmer for 1 minute
  3. Add a small amount of water to the cornstarch and stir so no lumps remain
  4. Add the now liquid cornstarch to the sauce and let it simmer a minute more
  5. let cool and use for whatever you want
